CubeSAT

For lack of a launch vehicle for our CubeSAT to piggyback on, the Cal CubeSAT Design Team has been temporarily put on hold.
Our team has previously developed a prototype 1 Unit CubeSat, for pedagogical purposes. This prototype CubeSat used amateur radio to communicate to a ground station in Berkeley. It is equipped with various sensors and a small camera. Development efforts focused on the command and data handling subsystem (C&DH), the power subsystem, and the communications subsystem.

Questions? Contact cubesat [at] aiaa.berkeley.edu
